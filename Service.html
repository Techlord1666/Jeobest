Our Specialized Services

Precision-engineered aluminum and glass solutions for residential and commercial buildings in Lagos and beyond.

Aluminum Windows

Top-quality sliding, casement, and fixed aluminum windows. We ensure airtight sealing to prevent leaks and reduce external noise.

Aluminum Doors

Strong and elegant entryways. We fabricate heavy-duty aluminum swing and sliding doors with premium lock systems for maximum security.

Aluminum Roof

Durable roofing solutions including long-span aluminum and stone-coated sheets. Built to withstand the harsh Nigerian sun and heavy rainfall.

Aluminum Partition

Professional office and indoor partitioning. We create sound-insulated workspaces with sleek aluminum frames and glass panels.

Frameless Glass Toilet

Modern tempered frameless glass shower cubicles and toilet dividers. Adds a luxurious and clean feel to your bathroom space.

Frameless Rainy's Staircase

Expertly crafted frameless glass railings and staircase finishing. We use heavy-duty tempered safety glass for structural integrity and beauty.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an installation take?

Most window projects take 7 to 14 working days from mobilization.

Yes, our technical team visits sites anywhere in Lagos to take precision measurements.

Is your glass tempered?

Yes, we strictly use high-grade tempered safety glass (10mm - 15mm) for all frameless work.

Business Policies

1. Material Quality

We use 6063-T5 architectural grade aluminum. We do not compromise on quality for cheaper alternatives.

2. Payment Terms

Mobilization requires a 70% deposit. The final 30% balance is payable immediately upon completion of installation.

3. Warranty

We provide a 12-month structural warranty covering leakages and moving parts failure under normal use.

4. Project Timeline

Estimated timelines are provided upon mobilization. We strive for prompt delivery on every project.

5. Refunds

Due to custom fabrication, deposits are non-refundable once materials have been purchased or cut to size.
