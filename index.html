Joe Best Aluminum Business Policies

1. Material Quality Standard

We strictly use high-grade 6063-T5 architectural aluminum profiles. All glass used is tempered/safety glass unless otherwise specified by the client for specific budget requirements.

2. Payment & Mobilization

A mobilization fee of 70% of the total contract sum is required before the procurement of materials. The remaining 30% is payable immediately upon the successful completion of the installation.

3. Project Timeline

Delivery dates are estimated based on the scope of work. Standard window installations typically take 7-14 working days from the date of mobilization. Joe Best Aluminum will not be held liable for delays caused by site unreadiness.

4. Warranty & Maintenance

We provide a 12-month structural warranty on all our installations. This covers leakages and moving parts failure. Damage caused by third-party construction workers or accidents is not covered under this warranty.

5. Variations & Refunds

Any changes to dimensions or designs after materials have been cut will attract additional costs. Due to the custom nature of aluminum fabrication, deposits are non-refundable once materials have been purchased.
