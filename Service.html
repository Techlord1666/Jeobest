Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an installation take?

Most residential window projects take between 7 to 14 working days from mobilization.

Do you offer home visits for measurements?

Yes, our technical team visits sites in Lagos and surroundings to take precision measurements before fabrication.

Is your glass tempered?

Yes, we strictly use tempered safety glass for all frameless installations to ensure maximum durability.

Business Policies

1. Material Quality

We use 6063-T5 architectural grade aluminum. We do not compromise on profile thickness.

2. Payment Terms

Mobilization requires a 70% deposit. The final 30% is payable upon completion of installation.

3. Warranty

We provide a 12-month structural warranty covering leakages and moving parts failure.

4. Refunds

Due to custom fabrication, deposits are non-refundable once materials are purchased/cut.
