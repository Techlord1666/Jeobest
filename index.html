Business Policies

1. Material Quality Standard

We strictly use high-grade 6063-T5 architectural aluminum profiles. All glass is tempered safety glass unless otherwise requested.

2. Payment & Mobilization

A mobilization fee of 70% is required before procurement. The balance of 30% is payable immediately upon installation.

3. Project Timeline

Standard installations take 7-14 working days. Delays caused by site unreadiness are not the liability of Joe Best Aluminum.

4. Warranty & Maintenance

We provide a 12-month structural warranty covering leakages and hardware failure under normal usage.

5. Variations & Refunds

Changes after material cutting attract extra costs. Deposits are non-refundable once materials are purchased/cut due to their custom nature.
